About Tim

I’m an dataviz and multimedia designer from Washington, D.C. Currently, I am the design and visuals manager at the Urban Institute, a non-partisan social and economic research institution. At Urban, I lead a team of designers that distill complex information into visually digestible presentations. My team and I have lead the intstitute into a new age of visual storytelling, while updating the organizations visual brand identity as well as the web site.

Previous to my time at Urban, I was the Creative Director at the NEED Project, a small non-profit that works with K-12 students across the country, teaching them about energy — everything ranging from wind and natural gas to the physics of light, sound and motion.

Before moving to Washington, I was an Infographics Artist at The Columbus Dispatch, a major metropolitan newspaper and central Ohio’s most popular web site. I started at The Dispatch in 2007 as I was finishing my Master’s degree in Photography at Ohio University’s School of Visual Communication. My master’s project explored the impact of multimedia on the design of national news web sites during the 2008 presidential election.

My backgrounds in computer science, art, design, and journalism have provided me with a unique set of skills that keep me on the cutting edge of new media technology and visual communication. I’m very quick to adapt to any new situation and I’m a voracious learner. I am detail oriented, a clear and concise editor, and cool under deadline. I’ve been a leader at every step in my career, whether it was by creating my own Digital Imaging major at Transylvania University (which was later adopted as a standard major), teaching multiple courses at Ohio University, or by being selected to be a member of the Digital Futures Group at The Columbus Dispatch, helping shape the future of journalism. Now I lead Urban Institute's visuals group, and set visual standards for the entire organization.

I’m happily married to my wife, Leslie, a Veterinarian practicing in Arlington, Virginia. We recently had our first child, Eleanor, who keeps us on our toes. We’ve got two dogs, Bonnie and Vernon, and two cats, Trudie and Charlie. I love to cook anything that takes more than six hours to make: I’ve won awards for my chili recipe, I’m an avid backyard barbecue enthusiast, and have recently started brewing my own beer.

Cheers!
